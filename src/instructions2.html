How to Play
During the game you can collect rings to get points, hearts to get back lives and shields to get invulnerability for some time. Eggman your enemy! He is coming for you, you will be warned about him with a red signal on the ground which moves along his trajectory so that you can avoid him even when he's behind you. You loose hearts when you hit an obstacle or when Eggman hits you. After you've been hit you're invulnerable for some seconds, use that time to escape from every danger! In the upper left corner you can see your score, in the upper right corner you can see your hearts. When you loose one or more hearts, the corresponding number of them turn gray

Select the difficulty of your game. An easy game allows you to get hit four times before a game over; the extreme game gives you only one shot.
