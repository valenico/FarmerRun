Watch out for you mortal enemy, Eggman! When he is coming for you, you will hear him and you will be warned with a red signal on the ground which moves along his trajectory so that you can avoid him even when he's behind you. He will repeatedly try to hit you with his huge weapon, to escape the danger you need to dodge left or right at the right time!
Your friend has been corrupted, Eggman has taken Tails by his side! Don't be fooled and dodge him, or he is gonna hit you hard.
The evil scientist has developed a new weapon to beat you, this robot will be targeting you over and over, you will be warned by his red laser. Pay attention, those projectiles are very fast!

Pick up this shield to gain invulnerability for some time, no one will be able to hurt you, but be careful: the shield won't last long.
You loose hearts when you hit an obstacle or when Eggman hits you. After you've been hit you're invulnerable for some seconds, use that time to escape from every danger! When you drop to 0 hearts it will be game over, taking a heart will restore one of them letting you to absorb one more hit from the enemies!
Your goal is to get as much score as you can collecting these coins. The coins will never be isolated but they spawn in rows, so go with the flow of the run to get them all! You can see your current score in the upper left corner.
